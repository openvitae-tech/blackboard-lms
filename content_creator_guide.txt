Module: Getting Started with Content Studio

Lesson: How to Create a Course Using Content Studio

Content Studio is an AI-powered course creation tool available to managers who have been granted creator permission. It works by reading a document you provide and automatically generating a structured video course from that content. To start, open Content Studio from the main navigation and click "New Course".

The wizard opens on an upload screen where you attach the source document for your course. Accepted formats include PDF, Word documents, plain text, and images. Once your file is attached, click "Next: Configure Video Style" to move to the branding screen, where you can upload your organisation's logo to appear in the generated videos. Click "Generate Course" and the system will begin building the course in the background.

A progress screen confirms the AI is working. This may take a few minutes. When it finishes, you are taken directly to the Course Structure screen. The AI always generates at least one module containing at least one lesson from your document. Each lesson row displays a status icon on the right: a spinning circle means the video is still being rendered, a clock means the lesson is queued, and a play icon means the video is ready.

Lesson: Reviewing and Editing Lessons

Click any lesson title on the Course Structure screen to open it. The Lesson page has three columns. On the left is a scene playlist where each thumbnail represents one short clip in the lesson. Click a thumbnail to load that scene in the video player at the centre of the page.

Below the video player you will see the scene script, which is the narration the AI wrote for that clip. You can edit this text directly. Once you have made your changes, click "Regenerate Scene" to re-render the clip with your updated narration. Each script is limited to 512 characters per scene.

When you have reviewed all scenes and are satisfied with the lesson, click "Verify and Next" at the bottom right to mark the lesson as verified.

Lesson: Saving and Publishing Your Course

After verifying all lessons, return to the Course Structure screen. Once every lesson shows the verified status, the "Save Course" button becomes active. Click "Save Course" to save the completed course to the LMS. The course is then available from the managing courses section where it can be published and assigned to learners.
